New Roots is more than just a marketplace; it's a community dedicated to connecting you directly with local farmers. Founded to support those whose farms were impacted by the events of October 7th, our platform offers the freshest, seasonal produce while directly supporting the individuals who cultivate it.

Join us in strengthening our community and enjoying the taste of home-grown goodness. By choosing New Roots, you're not just buying food; you're investing in the resilience of local agriculture and helping to plant the seeds for a sustainable future.

Our Story

The idea for New Roots was born out of a desire to create a stronger bond between farmers and consumers. We believe that everyone deserves access to fresh, locally sourced produce, and that farmers deserve fair compensation for their hard work.

Following the devastating events of October 7th, the need for this connection became even more urgent. Many farms faced unprecedented challenges, and New Roots emerged as a way to provide a lifeline, ensuring their produce reaches the tables of those who appreciate it.
